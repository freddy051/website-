Mission Statement

Dear Students,

At elite universities, students are often told that there are only a few set paths to success, and that deviating from these paths puts you at a disadvantage...

However, many students fail to recognize the inherent value in simply being at a top-tier university and having a solid resume...

  • It is possible to get a prestigious finance job with no networking and no prior finance experience.
  • Companies aren’t looking to hire Albert Einstein. They’re hiring a 23-year-old kid. The bar for excellence is not that high.
  • You may not actually end up being happy with the job you’ve been working so hard to get.
  • You could have a 2.5 GPA and a thin resume and 99% of entry-level jobs would still consider you.

I work with students at every stage of their college journey...

Sincerely,
Harold Bergner
