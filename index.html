Mission Statement

Dear Students,

At elite universities, students are often told that there are only a few set paths to success...

However, many students fail to recognize the inherent value in simply being at a top-tier university...

While university career counselors can offer valuable guidance, they often present the recruitment process as a formula...

What I offer to students that university career counselors cannot is context — context that is impossible to have while going through the process...

  • It is possible to get a prestigious finance job with no networking and no prior finance experience.
  • Companies aren’t looking to hire Albert Einstein. They’re hiring a 23-year-old kid.
  • You may not actually end up being happy with the job you’ve been working so hard to get.
  • You could have a 2.5 GPA and still be considered for 99% of entry-level jobs.

I work with students at every stage of their college journey...

And remember — you got this!

Sincerely,
Harold Bergner
